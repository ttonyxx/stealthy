Our Mission

We believe that healthcare costs should be transparent, predictable, and fair. Too many people are surprised by massive medical bills after receiving care. We're changing that by giving patients the power to compare prices, understand their options, and make informed decisions about their healthcare.

Stealthy started as a class project but quickly became our passion. We've interviewed hundreds of patients, analyzed thousands of medical bills, and built a platform that we wish existed when we needed it most.
